About Me

I am a 17-year old French American girl, born and raised in California. I live in Silver Lake, a tightly-knit neighborhood of Los Angeles, with my parents, 15-year old sister, 10-year old brother and two adorable cats. My siblings and I live in a bilingual household, with my French mother speaking exclusively french to us, and my American father speaking exclusively english to us. Growing up in a bicultural household has deeply shaped my personal identity and inspired me during sophomore year to move to France and spend 10th grade in La Rochelle, France with my great-grandmother. This experience was very challenging but ultimately the most amazing and rewarding experience of my life. Adapting to this new environment gave me the opportunity to venture out of my comfort zone and learn a lot about myself, my interests and my aspirations. A striking aspect of my time in France was the period it occurred in. I arrived in France two months before both the yellow vest movement and the environmental protests began. Learning about the purposes and values of each movement- whether I supported them or not- offered me insight into the way that the French political system works as well as the values upheld by its citizens. Experiencing the impact of these movements firsthand and the other experiences I had during my year abroad has made me realize that I am deeply interested in studying political science and global relations at university.

I am very passionate about environmental activism. I believe it is the responsibility of my generation to take charge of global warming and fight to save our planet. To educate myself and learn more about environmental activism, I have been participating in environmental clubs and projects at my various high schools since sophomore year. I hope to participate in similar and more impactful ventures in college.

Some of my less intense but favorite hobbies include reading, drawing, coding and learning Spanish and Mandarin. I also hope to continue these throughout my years in college.
